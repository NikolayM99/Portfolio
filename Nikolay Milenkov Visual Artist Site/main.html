Outgoing, positive and adaptive are some of the best words that can describe me. I take every task seriously and work hard to achieve it with the best possible results. Skills:

Experimenting with a variety of digital craft methods and tools has enriched my knowledge to a high degree. Of course, I continue to learn and I stay aware of new technologies that may appear more effective and which would enable me to accomplish work on a whole new level.

I have experience with photo and video editing, as well as digital sketching. At the same time, I consider myself competent regarding 3D modelling and sculpting, familiar with both hard surface and organic modeling workflows. Moreover, I can also work on the textures of my models. Rigging and animating are another processes that I have practice, although there is still a lot of room for improvement. My goal for the future is to continue improving my skills and further excel in the world of visual arts!

Stormtrooper Helmet

This project is to demonstrate that I know how to create a smooth and practical hard surface model and, also, that I can design with UVs and textures.

I worked with a number of references and as a result, I managed to build a good general shape, with colors and additional details which were my artistic choice.

It was satisfying for me to accomplish this particular task. My approach was to use practical knowledge, gained from previous projects and to upgrade it, in order to produce this impressive model.

X-Wing Starfighter

George Lucas movies are a great source of inspiration for me! That is why it should not surprise you to find another Star Wars-related project in my portfolio.

Let me present you the iconic X-Wing Starfighter! Powerful, yet elegant, this star ship is favorite to many. The task here was to use my existing hard surface modelling skills and enhance them. Making such a complex model, rich in tiny details, was challenging to me. I consider working on this model as a very educational experience, since it helped me optimize my time management abilities and taught me how to speed up the working process.

Although I am satisfied with my work on the X-Wing Starfighter, I plan to modify it in near future, by adding more details and textures.

Shield Maiden

Now, this is the most complex and labor-intensive project I have completed so far. My goal was to demonstrate competency in creating an accomplished character, from the first stroke to the last touch. Before start working on the actual design, I first prepared myself by doing a historical and artistic research, related to thumbnails and overpaints, modeling and sculpting, posing, and everything necessary to produce a completed character.

The idea came primarily from the TV series "Vikings". The character Lagertha was a real ispiration for me. I wanted to create a female character, that is strong and fearless, but at the same time still feminine. As far as aesthetics and style are concerned, I was aiming at something like "Overwatch", but still making something unique and distinguished.

Overall I am satisfied with the result of this challenging project, but I realize, that there is still room for improvement. Without a doubt my next characters will be better!
